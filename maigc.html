The M.A. in Integrated Global Communication at Kennesaw State University is a professional-oriented, 33-hour, four semester graduate program that features:

  1. The only global communication masters program in the Southeast
  2. A location in one of the nation's most vibrant global business and media centers
  3. An innovative curriculum that offers a balance of theory and skills important for a professional masters program
  4. A cohort model that builds collaboration and leadership skills
  5. A summer international experience where students will be immersed in foreign cultures, study global organizations or attend classes at a foreign institution
  6. A capstone experience that features a team-oriented professional project for a global client
